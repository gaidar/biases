# 33 – Authority bias

**Category:** D – Social and group dynamics

## Definition

Overweighting the opinion of the most senior or highest-status person, independent of the evidence.

## Impact on decisions

The HiPPO – highest-paid person's opinion – overrides data. Discussion ends when the senior-most voice speaks. Teams optimize for anticipating the executive's preference rather than for being right.

## Detection signals

- Decisions finalize immediately after the senior person's comment
- Test data quietly shelved when it contradicts an executive view
- Pre-meetings held to align with the boss before the real meeting

## Countermeasures

- Senior people speak last in evaluations
- Evaluate options blind where possible – strip authorship from proposals
- Establish that data presented before opinions is the standard order
- Executives should explicitly invite and reward disagreement

## Marketing example

The CEO prefers campaign concept A. Testing shows concept B converts 40% better with the target audience. The team runs A. Nobody presents the test data in the final meeting because "the decision was already made upstairs." The 40% delta was worth roughly $300K in pipeline.
